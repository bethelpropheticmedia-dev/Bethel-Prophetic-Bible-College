Our Vision

To raise true prophets and proclaim the divine revelation of God to the nations.

Our Mission

To train believers to become prophetic voices who boldly declare God’s Word and walk faithfully in His calling.

Prophetic Training & Ministry

Our college is committed to equipping students as prophetic trumpets who can hear, discern, and communicate the voice of God with wisdom, humility, and spiritual maturity.

  • Competency
  • Integrity
  • Accountability
  • Our Goal

Competency

Developing a strong foundation in

  • Strong foundation in the Word of God
  • Training in prophetic ministry
  • Knowledge in ministry disciplines
  • Spiritual leadership development

Integrity

Encouraging students to

  • Walk closely with God
  • Live a holy and disciplined life
  • Reflect Christ in character and ministry

Accountability

Training ministers to remain

  • Accountable to the Lord
  • Faithful to the Church
  • Respectful toward fellow ministers
  • Committed to biblical and ethical ministry practices

Our Goal

Our goal is to prepare end-time prophets who

  • Hear the voice of God
  • Understand divine revelation
  • Walk in obedience
  • Minister with spiritual wisdom and power
  • Impact nations through prophetic ministry

End-Time Prophetic Calling

We believe God is raising a generation of believers who will:

  • See what God reveals
  • Speak what God declares
  • Stand boldly for truth
  • Prepare the Church for the coming days
